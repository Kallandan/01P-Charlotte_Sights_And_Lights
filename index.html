RESTAURANTS

Charlotte has a wide variety of restaurant options that are sprinkled all over the city. From the high end restuarants, to authentic international restuartans, to the smaller family-owned diners. Charlottte has all the options to satisfy your appetite.

PARKS

When you need a breath of fresh air, Charlotte has plenty of grassy parks and green spaces. Whether you’re looking to get your heart pumping or just want to soak up an extra dose of vitamin D, these spots will get you a little more acquainted with Mother Nature.

With amusement parks, art museums, soulful entertainment, exciting sports, delicious dining & more, you'll never run out of things to do in the Queen City.
